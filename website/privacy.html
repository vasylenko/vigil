Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Vigil - Stay Awake ("Vigil") is a macOS menu bar utility developed by Serhii Vasylenko. Your privacy is straightforward because Vigil does not interact with the internet in any way.

Data Collection

Vigil does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reporting
  • No network requests of any kind
  • No user accounts or authentication
  • No third-party SDKs or frameworks

Local Storage

Vigil stores your preferences (sleep mode, launch-at-login setting, and last active state) locally on your Mac using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

System Permissions

Vigil uses macOS power assertions (IOPMAssertion) to prevent idle sleep. This is the same API used by macOS itself and does not require any special permissions or access to personal data.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact vigil@vasylenko.info.
