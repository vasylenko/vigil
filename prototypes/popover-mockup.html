Design Decisions

  • Accent color: Amber/gold (#e89a2e) — warm beacon tone replaces generic system blue. Used for active toggle and hero tint.
  • App icon: Real Vigil app icon (base64-embedded 128@2x PNG) with 16px corner radius, matching the SwiftUI clipShape.
  • Active state: warm amber tint on hero background + radial glow from icon center (beacon metaphor). Icon at full opacity.
  • Inactive state: no tint, icon drops to 35% opacity, lighthouse rendered in neutral gray with no rays — the beacon is "off".
  • Translucency: backdrop-filter: blur(60px) saturate(180%) on both themes — mimics macOS vibrancy materials.
  • Typography: SF Pro via -apple-system. Headline (600 weight) for "Stay Awake", body (400) for settings, caption (11px 400) for descriptions. No font-size variance beyond what the hierarchy requires.
  • Spacing: 280px width preserved. Generous internal padding; hero section has breathing room around the icon. Sections separated by 1px dividers with horizontal inset.
  • Controls: macOS-style toggle switches (38×22 hero, 32×18 settings) with amber "on" state. Segmented picker with inset selection and subtle shadow.
  • Dark mode: Not inverted — independently designed. Darker amber glow, brighter lighthouse strokes, adjusted secondary text to #98989d.
  • The popover arrow is a rotated square pseudo-element, matching macOS MenuBarExtra window style.
  • Menu bar preview strip shows the beacon icon at full vs. dimmed opacity, matching VigilApp.swift's 1.0/0.4 behavior.
